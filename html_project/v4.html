Wind Speed

A region's prevailing and dominant winds are enacted by global patterns of movement in the Earth's atmosphere. In general, easterly flow occurs at low and medium latitudes globally.In the mid-latitudes, westerly winds are the rule and their strength is largely determined by the polar cyclone.
